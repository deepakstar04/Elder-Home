what is assisted living?

Assisted living is a type of senior care that provides support with daily activities while promoting independence and quality of life. It offers a safe and comfortable environment for seniors who may need help with tasks such as bathing, dressing, medication management, and meal preparation. Assisted living facilities also provide social activities, transportation, and access to healthcare services, allowing residents to maintain an active and fulfilling lifestyle.
